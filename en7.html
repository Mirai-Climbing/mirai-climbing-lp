Features

Log

Gym and outdoor, all in one place

Record routes, notes, and videos so you can look back later.

  • Manage gym and outdoor logs together
  • Keep everything easy to review
Search

Find what you need, fast

Spend less time digging through logs and posts—focus on climbing.

  • Filter by keyword or conditions
  • Jump to the entries you want
Badges

See your progress at a glance

Earn badges for consistency and goals to stay motivated.

  • Visible streaks and achievements
  • Review growth on your profile
Partner finder

Turn “I want to climb with someone” into plans

Post by date, location, or style and connect with others.

  • Find partners and meetups
  • We’ll prioritize based on your feedback
Goals

Set goals and track progress

Define goals and see your progress so “keeping at it” gets easier.

  • Set goals and visualize progress
  • UI designed to make you want to check back
Knowledge

Safety, terms, and tips at your fingertips

Quick access to basics and safety notes from within the app.

  • Glossary and basics
  • Safety and best practices
